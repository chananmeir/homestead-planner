# Homestead Planner — Site Review

## What Is It?

Homestead Planner is a comprehensive, all-in-one web application for anyone planning a garden, managing a small farm, or building out a full homestead. It covers the entire lifecycle — from planning what to grow, to placing seeds in the ground, to tracking your harvest and livestock production.

---

## Garden Planning & Design

**Season Planner** — The heart of the app. You create an annual garden plan, pick your crops, set quantities, and assign them to beds. The planner handles:

- **Automatic space calculations** across four planting methods: Square-Foot Gardening, MIGardener seed-density, Intensive spacing, and traditional Row planting.
- **Succession planting** — schedule staggered plantings so you get continuous harvests rather than everything at once.
- **Crop rotation checking** — warns you if you're planting the same family in a bed too soon, based on 3-year history.
- **Nutrition estimates** — see how many calories and grams of protein your plan will produce.
- **Bed assignment** — allocate crops across multiple beds with even or custom distribution.
- **Export to calendar** — push your plan into the planting calendar with one click.

**Visual Garden Designer** — An interactive grid where you drag-and-drop plants into your beds. Features include:

- Click or drag to place individual plants on a grid matching your bed dimensions.
- Real-time **conflict detection** — the app warns you if placements overlap or violate spacing rules.
- Support for **trellis planting** (tomatoes, cucumbers, grapes on linear structures).
- **Seed density modes** — broadcast sowing, row-based sowing with thinning, and traditional single-plant spacing.
- **Future plantings overlay** — preview where your upcoming succession plantings will go.
- **Seed saving** — mark plants for seed collection, track maturity dates, and log when seeds are harvested.
- Status tracking through the full lifecycle: planned, seeded, transplanted, growing, harvested.

**Property Designer** — A bird's-eye canvas of your entire property. Place garden beds, structures (coops, greenhouses, sheds, barns), fruit/nut trees, and trellises on a scaled map. Snap-to-grid placement, rotation, custom sizing, and a library of 50+ pre-built structure types.

---

## Seed Management

**Personal Inventory** — Track every seed packet you own: variety, brand, quantity, expiration, germination rate, purchase info, and price. Includes 14 agronomic override fields (days to maturity, spacing, temperature tolerances, bolt resistance, flavor profile, storage rating) so you can record real-world performance that differs from catalog data.

**Seed Catalog** — A built-in plant database you can browse and clone entries from into your personal inventory. Bulk **CSV import** is supported for adding packets in batch.

---

## Growing & Scheduling

**Planting Calendar** — Three views (list, grid, timeline) showing your full planting schedule across the season. Log garden events like mulching, fertilizing, irrigation, and even maple tapping season. Filter by bed, date range, or crop.

**Indoor Seed Starts** — Track seeds from sowing through germination to hardening off and transplanting. Log growing conditions (light hours, temperature, location), germination rates, and transplant readiness. Directly transplant into the garden designer when ready.

**Soil Temperature Card** — Displays current and forecast soil temps at 5/10/15/20 C thresholds, helping you know when conditions are right for direct sowing.

---

## Weather & Environment

Pulls weather data for your location: current conditions, 7-day forecast, frost alerts, freeze warnings, heat advisories, and wind/rain alerts. Displays your USDA hardiness zone, last/first frost dates, and UV index. Configured by zip code with persistent storage.

---

## Harvest Tracking

Log every harvest: date, crop, weight or count, quality rating (excellent/good/fair/poor), and notes. View aggregate statistics — total yield per plant, harvest counts, average quality, and seasonal totals. Link harvests to specific planted items for full traceability.

---

## Nutrition Dashboard

Aggregates nutritional data across your entire operation:

- **Garden crops** — estimated calories, protein, carbs, and fat based on planned yield.
- **Livestock production** — egg and honey contributions.
- **Fruit & nut trees** — annual yield from trees placed on your property.
- Supports importing USDA FoodData Central entries or adding custom nutrition data.

This answers the question: *"Can my homestead feed my family?"*

---

## Livestock Management

**Chickens & Ducks** — Individual animal records (name, breed, age, status). Log daily egg production with quantity and quality ratings.

**Beehives** — Track hive health with structured inspections (queen sighting, brood pattern, food stores). Log honey harvests with quantity and container type.

**Other Livestock** — Generic tracking for goats, pigs, sheep, or any animal. Health records with treatment logging and outcome tracking.

---

## Compost Tracker

Create and monitor compost piles: dimensions, ingredients (green/brown with C:N ratios), temperature, moisture level, and status progression from building through cooking, curing, and ready. Tracks when piles were last turned and estimates readiness.

---

## Photo Gallery

Upload and categorize photos of your garden, harvests, plants, and property. Link photos to specific beds, plants, or planted items for documentation over time. Useful for tracking progress, identifying pests/diseases, and keeping a visual journal.

---

## Multi-User & Administration

Supports multiple user accounts with full data isolation — every user sees only their own data. Admin panel for user management (add, edit, delete, password reset, promote/demote admin).

---

## Who Is This For?

| User Type | What They'll Use Most |
|---|---|
| **Backyard gardener** | Garden Designer, Planting Calendar, Seed Inventory |
| **Square-foot gardener** | Season Planner (SFG method), automatic spacing, succession planting |
| **Market gardener** | Row/Intensive methods, harvest tracking, nutrition dashboard |
| **Homesteader** | Everything — property layout, livestock, compost, nutrition self-sufficiency analysis |
| **Seed saver** | Seed inventory with agronomic overrides, seed saving lifecycle on planted items |

---

## Strengths

- **Integrated lifecycle** — plan, schedule, place, grow, harvest, and track nutrition all in one place.
- **Four planting methods** with synchronized space calculations so estimates are accurate regardless of your gardening style.
- **Succession planting** is a first-class feature, not an afterthought — staggered plantings, conflict detection, and calendar export all work together.
- **Nutrition analysis** ties garden output to actual food production, answering the self-sufficiency question.
- **Property-level view** brings together beds, structures, trees, and livestock infrastructure on one map.
- **Seed-to-harvest traceability** — track a plant from the seed packet, through indoor starting, transplanting, growing, harvesting, and even seed saving for next year.

---

## Bottom Line

Homestead Planner covers the full spectrum of what a gardener or homesteader needs to manage: soil to table, seeds to livestock, planning to harvest. It's not just a garden layout tool — it's a production management system for anyone trying to grow food seriously.
