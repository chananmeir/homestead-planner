Eliot Coleman's Tips
  • Persephone Period: Late Oct - Mid Feb, daylight < 10 hours
  • Plant in late summer to reach maturity before this period
  • Layer system: Each layer = ~15°F protection
  • Best crops: Spinach, mâche, claytonia, kale, leeks
Nico Jabour's Techniques
  • Quick hoops: 10' wire bent into hoops with row cover
  • Snow as insulation - don't remove it!
  • Plant cold-hardy crops late Aug/early Sept
  • Harvest outer leaves, leaving center to regenerate
